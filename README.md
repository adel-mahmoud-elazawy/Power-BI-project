# 📊 Supermarket Sales Analysis Dashboard

## 📝 Project Description
This project is a comprehensive **Power BI Dashboard** created to analyze supermarket sales data. The goal is to transform raw sales data into actionable insights, helping business owners understand branch performance, product line popularity, and consumer behavior trends during the first quarter of 2019.

## 🚀 Key Features
* **Executive Summary (KPIs):** High-level view of Total Sales, Gross Income, and Cost of Goods Sold (COGS).
* **Time Series Analysis:** A line chart visualizing the sales and profit trends across January, February, and March.
* **Product Performance:** Bar charts comparing product lines by quantity sold and gross income.
* **Demographic & Geographic Insights:** Analysis of sales distribution by City (Mandalay, Yangon, Naypyitaw) and Customer Gender.
* **Interactive Filters:** Month-based slicers for dynamic data exploration.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Tools
* **Power BI Desktop:** Dashboard design and visualization.
* **Power Query:** Data cleaning, transformation, and handling missing values.
* **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions):** Created custom measures for financial calculations.

## 📈 Key Insights
1.  **February Dip:** There is a noticeable decrease in sales during February compared to January and March.
2.  **Top Category:** The "Food and Beverages" product line is the most profitable and has the highest sales volume.
3.  **Regional Leader:** Branch C in Naypyitaw city leads in terms of total revenue.
4.  **Customer Balance:** Sales are almost equally distributed between Male and Female customers, with a slight edge for Females.
